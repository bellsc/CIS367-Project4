Project 4: Simple Game in  WebGL - An extension of the Helicopter animation from Project 3
@author Scott Bell

Keyboard controls:

Helicopter "realistic" control
    W: Tilt helicopter forward
    S: Tilt helicopter backward
    A: Tilt helicopter left
    D: Tilt helicopter right
    Q: Rotate helicopter left
    E: Rotate helicopter right

    X: Increase propeller speed
    Z: Decrease propeller speed

Lighting control
    9: Toggle helicopter spotlight
    0: Toggle sunlight

Camera control (hold SHIFT)
    W: Camera look up
    S: Camera look down
    A: Camera look left
    D: Camera look right
    Q: Camera rotate left
    E: Camera rotate right
